How To Climb A V1 In The Gym

Instructions

Picking a Route

  1. Find the gym's route setting board
  2. Determine what color tape or handholds equate a route to V1
  3. Find a V1 on the wall that looks fun

Beginning the Route

  1. Place hands and/or feet on the indicated starting holds
  2. CLIMB!
  3. Place both hands on the last hold to complete the route
  4. Jump down, landing on feet and rolling backwards to dispell your momentum
